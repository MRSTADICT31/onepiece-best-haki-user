Strongest Haki User in One Piece

5. Silvers Rayleigh

Silvers Rayleigh, known as the "Dark King," is one of the greatest Haki users in the world of One Piece. As the former first mate of Gol D. Roger, Rayleigh mastered all three types of Haki and demonstrated advanced control over them. He was able to use powerful Armament Haki to destroy objects from the inside and possessed exceptional Observation Haki that allowed him to sense enemies from great distances. Even in his old age, Rayleigh remained a terrifying fighter whose Haki alone made him a threat to powerful opponents.

3. Shanks

Red-Haired Shanks is widely regarded as one of the greatest Haki specialists in One Piece. Unlike many top-tier fighters, Shanks relies almost entirely on Haki rather than Devil Fruit powers. His Conqueror's Haki is so overwhelming that it can intimidate powerful enemies from incredible distances. Shanks has also demonstrated exceptional Observation Haki and Armament Haki, making him a complete fighter. His ability to project his presence across vast areas showcases a level of Haki mastery few characters can match.

2. Gol D. Roger

ol D. Roger, the Pirate King, reached the pinnacle of power without possessing a Devil Fruit ability. His legendary battles against the strongest figures of his era were driven primarily by his incredible Haki. Roger's Conqueror's Haki was powerful enough to clash with Whitebeard's without their weapons even touching. His mastery of Haki allowed him to conquer the Grand Line and achieve what no pirate had done before. Even decades after his death, Roger's Haki remains a benchmark for greatness.
